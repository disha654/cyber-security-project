Don't worry, your data was not actually stolen. This was a phishing simulation designed to help you recognize real-world cyber threats.

How to Spot a Real Phish:

  • Check the URL: Real login pages have official domains (e.g., login.microsoft.com). Always check the address bar.
  • Urgent Language: Scammers use "ACT NOW" or "Your account will be suspended" to make you panic.
  • Generic Greetings: Be wary of emails starting with "Dear Customer" instead of your name.
  • Look for Typos: Professional companies rarely have spelling or grammar mistakes in their login pages.

Stay safe and always double-check before entering your credentials!
